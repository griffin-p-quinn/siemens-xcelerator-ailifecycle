The target

Fly in 3.

The goal is not another point solution. It is one governed execution layer that can move an aircraft program from requirement to a flight-ready product through a continuous, agent-driven lifecycle.

Design

Turn intent into a verified product definition.

Requirements, systems engineering, simulation, electrical, mechanical, and embedded design stay connected to the original mission.

Realize

Turn the definition into a buildable product.

PLM, manufacturing simulation, and production execution receive governed context instead of disconnected handoff packages.

Optimize

Turn evidence into the next lifecycle decision.

Telemetry, verification, and certification close the loop so operational learning can inform the product continuously.
